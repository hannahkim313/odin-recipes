Baked Salmon

Description

This baked salmon recipe is perfect for those looking to cook a simple yet healthy meal. Packed with nutrition, this recipe is sure to fill anyone up while satisfying their cravings.

Ingredients

  • 4 salmon fillets (about 6 ounces each)
  • 2 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt (or to taste)
  • 1/4 teaspoon cracked black pepper (a pinch if finely ground pepper)
  • 2 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 teaspoon Italian herb seasoning blend
  • 1 medium lemon
