Tiramisu

Description

This tiramisu recipe is made for dessert lovers who want to enjoy a no-bake process and spend as little time as possible in the kitchen. Simple and easy to make, this recipe will surely satisfy those cravings.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 8 ounce mascarpone cheese (room temperature)
  • 1/3 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cup strong espresso (room temperature)
  • 1 package lady fingers
  • Cocoa powder for dusting
