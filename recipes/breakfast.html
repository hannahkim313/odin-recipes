Sheepherder's Breakfast

Description

This sheepherder's breakfast recipe is perfect for anyone looking to make something quick and delicious. As a one-dish casserole, this recipe can be served at any time of day for all parties.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 pound bacon strips, finely chopped
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 1 package (30 ounces) frozen shredded hash brown potatoes, thawed
  • 8 large eggs
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
